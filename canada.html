About Canada

Government: Federal Parliamentary Constitutional Monarchy.

Population: Approx. 38 Million.

Known for its polite citizens, bilingualism (English & French), and vast wilderness. Canada champions multiculturalism and has a rich history of Indigenous heritage.

Canada Food & Cuisine

Poutine

French fries and cheese curds topped with a brown gravy. A Quebec classic.

Maple Taffy

Hot maple syrup poured onto snow to harden into a sweet, sticky treat.

Must-See Places

Banff National Park

Rocky Mountain peaks, turquoise glacial lakes, and abundant wildlife.

Toronto / CN Tower

Canada's largest city, a bustling metropolis of culture and commerce.

Canadian National Anthem

"O Canada" is the national anthem of Canada. The lyrics were originally written by Sir Adolphe-Basile Routhier in 1880, with the English version finalized in 1908.

Official English Lyrics:
O Canada! Our home and native land!
True patriot love in all of us command.
With glowing hearts we see thee rise,
The True North strong and free!
From far and wide,
O Canada, we stand on guard for thee.

God keep our land glorious and free!
O Canada, we stand on guard for thee.
O Canada, we stand on guard for thee.

Additional Historical Verse:

Great prairies spread and lordly rivers flow.

From East to Western Sea;
